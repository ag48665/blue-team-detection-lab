# Lab04 – Phishing Investigation

## Objective

Investigate a phishing email, identify Indicators of Compromise (IOCs), analyze email headers, and document the incident from a Blue Team perspective.

---

## Scenario

A user reported a suspicious email requesting verification of their email account.

The objective was to determine whether the email was malicious, identify phishing indicators, and recommend mitigation actions.

---

## Tools Used

- Visual Studio Code
- Email (.eml) sample
- Header Analysis
- MITRE ATT&CK Framework
